Our Research Project

Project Overview

This page introduces our main project themes, focusing on the design and application of biotin-avidin-based planar assemblies.

Objectives

  • Develop a novel biotin-avidin surface patterning technique.
  • Visualize molecular interactions via fluorescence microscopy.
  • Establish interdisciplinary methods integrating biology and physics.

Team Members

Our team consists of undergraduate and graduate students in life science and applied physics at Tokyo Science University.
